About ONGC

India's Energy Pioneer

Oil and Natural Gas Corporation Limited (ONGC) is India's largest crude oil and natural gas company, contributing around 70% to Indian domestic production. Established in 1956, ONGC has grown to become a Fortune Global 500 company and a Maharatna PSU.

With operations spanning across India and 17 countries globally, ONGC is committed to energy security and sustainable development. Our integrated business model covers the entire hydrocarbon value chain from exploration to marketing.

We are dedicated to adopting clean and green technologies, reducing carbon footprint, and contributing to India's transition towards a low-carbon economy while ensuring energy security for the nation.
